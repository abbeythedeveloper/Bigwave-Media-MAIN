Our Services

Lighting design and Installation

BigWaveMediaConcept offers comprehensive lighting design services that can be customized to fit the unique needs of each client. Their team of experienced lighting designers will work with clients to create a lighting plan that will enhance the mood and ambiance of their event. The company also offers lighting installation services to ensure that the lighting design is executed flawlessly.

Event decoration

BigWaveMediaConcept offers a range of decoration services to help clients create the perfect atmosphere for their event. From floral arrangements to custom backdrops and centerpieces, the company's team of skilled designers can help bring any vision to life.

Audio and visual equipment rentals

BigWaveMediaConcept also provides audio and visual equipment rental services to help clients create a complete audiovisual experience. They offer a wide range of equipment, including projectors, screens, speakers, and microphones, to ensure that every aspect of the event is covered.

Events planning and co-ordination

In addition to lighting and decoration services, BigWaveMediaConcept also offers event planning and coordination services to help clients manage every aspect of their event. Their team of experienced event planners will work with clients to create a custom plan that covers everything from venue selection to catering and entertainment.

Event Space Ambience, Brand unveiling and stage lighting

Event Space Ambience, Brand Unveiling, and Stage Lighting are key factors to consider for a successful event. The ambiance of the space sets the tone, the brand unveiling must create a lasting impression, and stage lighting can set the mood and highlight important elements. By creating a cohesive and immersive experience for attendees, event organizers can effectively communicate their message and create a memorable event.

Outdoor fireworks

Outdoor fireworks can add excitement and spectacle to any event. From traditional displays to modern pyrotechnics, a well-executed fireworks show can captivate an audience and create an unforgettable experience. Careful planning, adherence to safety guidelines, and consideration of environmental factors are essential for a successful fireworks display. With the right approach, outdoor fireworks can be a dazzling addition to any event, leaving a lasting impression on attendees.
